Fringe

More than just a conference.

Here you can find information about parties and other events taking place around the same time as LXJS that you might be interested in attending.

Parties

We'll have three parties at the end of the day.

Welcome Party (October 1st)

Our welcome party, sponsored by appendTo, will be in our Official Hotel - Hotel Florida.

Hotel Florida is located steps away from the Marques de Pombal Underground Station, the hotel is close to many points of interest and to the conference venue. Party starts at 19h00 (7PM) and the address is :

Rua Duque de Palmela 34, Lisbon, 1250-098 PT


View Larger Map

Main Party (October 2nd)

Similarly to last year, and thanks to our sponsor Spotify, we'll have a boat cocktail - Yay! The boat will leave Alcantara docks at around 19h (or as soon as we all get there) so that we try and get some of the sunset's views right from the tagus river. Don't worry, we'll have buses leaving the venue (Teatro de S. Jorge) at around 18:30 to take you there. Unfortunately, because the boat can only carry around 200 persons, we do ask you to pre-register.

After the boat, at around 21h00 (9pm), we will, again, have a bus service taking you to the Venue (Teatro S. Jorge) in case you want to head home or your hotel with a stop on the way in the Rossio square to drop any of you that will want to continue the party on your own in Chiado / Bairro alto / Cais do Sodre - where a lot of the night life in Lisbon happens.

Farewell Party (October 3rd)

Our farewell party, sponsored by clubjudge will be at Ministerium. A club right in the center of Lisbon in Praca do Comercio. The party starts at around 20h00 (8pm).

To get there, you can hop in the subway (at Avenida metro station right in front of the Venue) and ride it to Terreiro do paco or you can just walk there and enjoy the main streets and squares of Lisbon. You can check the subway network here.

We will have groups of people leaving the venue, on foot, starting from 18h30 (6:30pm), so you can just come with us.

Nodecopter

Besides the impressive amount of partying, on October 4th (Friday, the day after the conference) there will be an all-day Nodecopter special event. Check the details on the Nodecopter website.

MongoDBLX Meetup September

If you're around for LXJS and you're interested in MongoDB, you can attend the monthly edition of the MongoDB meetup on the 30th of September. Just make sure you RSVP on their Meetup page.

http://www.meetup.com/Lisbon-MongoDB-User-Group/events/138564562/

Greenfest

Greenfest is a festival about sustainable development with tons of free activities like talks, workshops, movies, theatre, and many others. It's taking place from the 3rd to the 6th of October, so if you're interested in these subjects, go have a look at it!

http://www.greenfestival.pt/
