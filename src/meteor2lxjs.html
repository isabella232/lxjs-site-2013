Meteor 2 LXJS

Special batch of Student Tickets by Meteor.js

We measure the success of LXJS by how much we give back to the community. Opening up access to the conference to people who are still making their way is a powerful way to do that.

So thanks to the generosity of our friends at Meteor.js, we’re opening up a batch of super discounted student tickets (55€ + VAT)! If you’re a student who’s been watching the action around LXJS from afar and wishing you could get in on it, we want you here! And this is your chance...

How can you get your student ticket

To be eligible to win one of this tickets at a special price you have to:

  1. Be a student taking a MSc/BSc degree or in an High School
  2. Develop an Meteor.js app and make it publicly available, it can be any kind of app, even an “Hello World”, you just have to use Meteor.js

The contest will start on 17th of September at 7pm UTC+1, you should develop and deploy your Meteor.js app till then, after it starts, shout out a Tweet with: @lxjs, @meteorjs, a link to the app and the hashtag #Meteor2LXJS. After doing this send us an email to hello@lxjs.org with the link to the tweet and a proof that you are a student (e.g. photo of student card), we will offer 25 tickets at 55€+VAT* price for the first comers :)
* These tickets are non refundable
