Parties

We'll have three parties at the end of the day: Welcome Party (October 1st), Main Party (October 2nd) and the big Closing Party (October 3rd). Details TBA closer to the date, just make sure your schedule is free.

Nodecopter

Besides the impressive amount of partying, on October 4th (Friday, the day after the conference) there will be an all-day Nodecopter special event. Check the details on the Nodecopter website.

Greenfest

Greenfest is a festival about sustainable development with tons of free activities like talks, workshops, movies, theatre, and many others. It's taking place from the 3rd to the 6th of October, so if you're interested in these subjects, go have a look at it!
