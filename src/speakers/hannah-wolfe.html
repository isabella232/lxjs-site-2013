Speakers / Hannah Wolfe

Hannah Wolfe is a multi-disciplinary programmer who recently fell in love with JavaScript and node.js.

She has spent the past few years at MOO.com, working on all aspects of their website with a favouritism for site analytics, WordPress, frontend development and in particular modern JavaScript development techniques.

In her spare time, she co-founded the Ghost Foundation with John O'Nolan, and is now the CTO - heading up development on Ghost as well as several other supporting projects.
