Speakers / Dustin Diaz

Engineer, Photographer, and amateur Mixologist. Dustin Diaz is the Frontend architect at Change.org. Previously Diaz has worked at Yahoo, Google, Twitter, and the Obvious corp (Medium.com).

He's an author of two books; "JavaScript Design Patterns" and "This is Strobist® Info", and a co-creator of the Ender JavaScript package manager. He can also beat-box if you can flow.
