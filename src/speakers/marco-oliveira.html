Speakers / Marco Oliveira

Marco is a software engineer who has rediscovered JavaScript. His love affair with JavaScript started upon discovering node.js, and after years working with PHP.

Having been in the web development scene for more than a decade, he's currently working in Porto as CTO of Baboom Limited. Whenever possible, he enjoys posting pictures of food in social networks, hacking around, and contributing to the open source community.

One of the authors of the Twitter Bower rewritten architecture, and also co-founder of Indigo United, a group that is trying to push the web a bit further by creating tools that help developers create amazing applications.
