Speakers / Chris Bumgardner

Chris fell in love with Node.js in 2010 and never looked back, having built two startups and a handful of open source projects on a node stack. Currently Chris is CTO and CoFounder of Skycatch, where he is putting node to work in embedded robotics to control autonomous unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). He also loves kite boarding and flying airplanes. Check out his GitHub profile and follow him on Twitter.
