Speakers / Bill Mills

Dr. Bill Mills is a nuclear and particle physicist currently working at TRIUMF (Canada’s national lab for nuclear and particle physics) to modernize computing in his field, by building the next generation of experimental monitoring & control interfaces for the cutting edge of nuclear physics. Bill’s research has included record-setting new limits on new physics at the Large Hadron Collider at CERN, nuclear structure at Yale, and cancer radiotherapy at UBC.
